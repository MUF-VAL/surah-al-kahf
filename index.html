About Surah Al-Kahf

Surah Al-Kahf is the 18th chapter of the Holy Quran, consisting of 110 verses (ayat). It was revealed in Makkah and is highly recommended to be recited every Friday.

This blessed chapter provides protection from various trials, especially the Dajjal (Antichrist). The Prophet Muhammad (ï·º) said: "Whoever memorizes ten verses from the beginning of Surah Al-Kahf will be protected from the trial of the Dajjal."

The Surah contains four main stories that teach us about different types of trials: the trial of faith, wealth, knowledge, and power. Each story offers profound lessons for believers.
